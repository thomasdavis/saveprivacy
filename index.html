STOP THE SPIES

Australian Campaign Against Mass Surveillance

LAUNCH DATE: MID-LATE SEPTEMBER

Stop The Spies is a campaign to raise awareness about the Australian government's new data retention proposals. The website will launch in mid-late September with tools that allow citizens to directly reach out to their representatives. With your help we can halt the implementation of a surveillance state before it is too late.

What are we building?

Many developers, organizations and Redditors have poured hundreds of hours in over the past few weeks.

We're building new tools to open up democracy down under, by letting you:

  • Find your representatives with one click
  • Email, call, tweet and Facebook message your legislators
  • Share a short professional animation which explains the new laws in layman's terms
  • A campaign site with everything you need to learn about data retention.

These tools are all free software, meaning future campaigns for any issue will have an easier time putting constituents in direct contact with legislators

What needs to be done before launch?

On the first day of the campaign, all websites who sign up will show a banner showing how Australians can learn about data retention and tell their representatives what they think. Before then, we need to spread the word so that the official launch can reach as many people as possible in the first week.
